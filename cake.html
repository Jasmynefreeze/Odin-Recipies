Strawberry Short Cake

My mom made this for me as a kid. Its not intricate, but has good memories attached to it. If you are feeling lazy, but want something sweet I reccomend you try this!

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Sugar
  • Angel food cake
  • Vanilla Ice cream
  • Whipped cream
  1. Make angel food cake according to box instructions, or just buy it. I dont know how to make from scratch.
  2. Cut strawberries. Put in bowl, tossing the tops out.
  3. Pour a ton of sugar into the bowl holding strawberries. Mix and place in fridge, for atleast 20 minutes.
  4. Cut a serving of cake, placing it on plate.
  5. Place vanilla ice cream on top the cake.
  6. Top with strawberries from earlier and whipped cream.
